Shadowloop

Horror-themed first-person puzzles game

The player is stuck in a loop.
To get out of it, he will have to manipulate objects to cast the correct shadow and solve puzzles and advance through the house. But beware, you are not alone in the house...something in the shadows is waiting for your mistake.
Will you be able to break the loop and escape the house?

  • Languages: C++ / Blueprint

About Me

I am Mattia Carcaiso, a Game developer living in Italy, to be precise, in a small town in the lower Bergamo area. Land of fog and rough people. After many years in the IT consulting world as a software developer, I decided to fulfill the dream that drove me to study computer science as a child...to become a video game developer.
Besides being in front of the PC all day between developing and video games, in my spare time I also devote myself to another great passion of mine, which is theater. I am currently taking a theater acting class in the city where I live.
In the little spare time I have left, I enjoy reading books, watching movies, trying to have a social life and sleeping(although we all know that programmers never sleep)
